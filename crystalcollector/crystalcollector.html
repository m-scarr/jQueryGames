Crystal Collector


Instructions:


You will be given a random number at the start of the game, you're goal is to reach that number exactly. The target number is the large green number between the crystals, your current number is beneath it, in blue. Each crystal is also assigned a random value at the beginning of the game, and each time you click one, it's value is added to your current number. You can purchase a special gem whose value is always one, it's on sale for $19.99!
